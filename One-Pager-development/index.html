Collection Spotlight

Just Arrived

Spotlight Book

Hidden figures : the American dream and the untold story of the Black women mathematicians who helped win the space race by Margot Lee Shetterly
Dive into the book that has now become the top movie in the nation in January 2017! This fascinating and long overdue history tells the story of the African-American women who worked for the Langley Aeronautical Laboratory, in the early days of the space race. Though the West Computing Group were segregated from and paid much less than their white counterparts, their high quality work was crucial to the orbital missions of John Glenn and his fellow astronauts. If you loved the performances of Taraji Henson, Octavia Spencer, and Janelle Monae, grab the real story. Truth is always stranger, sadder, but often more inspiring than fiction.
